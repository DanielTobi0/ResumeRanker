Title: Senior Data Engineer

Company: Innovatech Solutions

Location: Remote (US)

Innovatech Solutions is seeking a motivated and experienced Senior Data Engineer to join our growing data platform team. You will be responsible for designing, building, and optimizing our data pipelines, making data a core asset for our product and business teams. This role requires a strong technical background and the ability to mentor other engineers.

Key Responsibilities:
Design, build, and maintain robust and scalable data pipelines using Python and SQL.
Develop ETL/ELT processes to ingest data from various sources into our cloud data warehouse.
Collaborate with data scientists and analysts to understand data needs and provide reliable datasets.
Ensure data quality, integrity, and reliability across our platform.
Mentor and guide junior data engineers, promoting best practices in code and design.

Minimum Qualifications:
5+ years of professional experience in a data engineering role.
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
Expert-level proficiency in Python and SQL.
Hands-on experience with at least one major cloud platform (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
Proven experience building and optimizing 'big data' data pipelines.

Preferred Qualifications:
Master's degree in a related field.
Experience with workflow orchestration tools like Apache Airflow.
Familiarity with streaming technologies such as Kafka or Kinesis.
Experience with big data tools like Apache Spark.
AWS Certified Data Analytics or similar certification is a plus.
Experience in a fast-paced SaaS environment.
